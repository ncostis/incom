A late 18th century olive oil mill located at the beautiful scenery of the Corfiot south east coast, an elegant structure of local architecture surrounded by the perennial blue of the Ionian Sea and sky, luscious bougainvillea and graceful olive trees. 
This is the heart of “The Olivar Suites” hotel, a special place to experience the finest of Corfiot hospitality and lasting sensations of wellbeing.
